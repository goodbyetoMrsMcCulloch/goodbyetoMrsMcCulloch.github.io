Skiing 2020


I went skiing in the February half term of 2020 and I really enjoyed it.
It was so fun speeding down the slopes.
You can find a couple of videos below.
At the ski resort I went to there was a train going literally up the mountain!.
It used coggs on the bottom of the train to interlock with special track so the train didn't hurtle down the mountainout of controll.
There was WiFi at lots of places so I could check my phone without paying Swiss roaming charges.
I used an app that told me how many lifts I'd taken and how far I had gone. It was really fun to be able to view all my skiing stats on the app.
